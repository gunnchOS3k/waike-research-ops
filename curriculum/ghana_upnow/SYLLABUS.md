# WAIKE Ghana UPNOW: Build, Ship, Serve — Syllabus

> **Conceptual only — not for construction**
> Expert review required before construction or field deployment.
> Requires licensed architect/engineer review before construction.


## Course spine (12 weeks)

| Week | Topic | Assignment | Lab |
|------|-------|------------|-----|
| 1 | Place, ethics, public benefit, and local accountability | A1: Place-based ethics and non-claim memo | L1: Local digital access map |
| 2 | Digital foundations, device readiness, and safety | A2: Learner device and access audit | L2: Network diagram and connectivity audit |
| 3 | Networking and connectivity (Wi-Fi, wired, cellular, edge, NTN where relevant) | A3: Connectivity and resilience worksheet | L3: Link budget or coverage model (where relevant) |
| 4 | Hardware, repair, sensors, safety, and maintenance | A4: Hardware, repair, and sensor safety plan | L4: Device repair, sensor, and hardware safety lab |
| 5 | Software, web, app foundations, and documentation | A5: Software or web project | L5: Lightweight website or dashboard |
| 6 | Cybersecurity, privacy, data protection, and threat modeling | A6: Cybersecurity and privacy tabletop | L6: Cyber hygiene tabletop |
| 7 | Cloud, AI literacy, edge AI, responsible AI, and evaluation | A7: AI, cloud, and edge workflow | L7: AI/cloud notebook or offline workflow |
| 8 | Site-specific applied systems | A8: Site-specific applied systems project | L8: Site-specific applied system lab |
| 9 | Campus/building design studio: room program, acoustics, RF, power, safety | A9: Room program and acoustic/RF tradeoff memo | L9: Building material matrix and scoring |
| 10 | Community project sprint | A10: Conceptual floor plan | L10: Acoustic/RF room boundary scoring |
| 11 | Portfolio, certifications, career/apprenticeship/scholarship readiness | A11: Final capstone report | L11: Floor plan adjacency graph |
| 12 | Final capstone demo and public/private presentation | A12: Final presentation deck outline | L12: Final integrated demo |
