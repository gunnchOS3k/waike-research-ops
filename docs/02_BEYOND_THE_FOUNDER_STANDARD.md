# Beyond the Founder Standard

WAIKE is **not** limited to what Edmund has personally taken in school. Edmund's education is the seed, not the ceiling.

## Five layers for every topic

1. Plain-English intuition
2. Academic foundation
3. Current industry standard
4. Vendor/tool reality
5. Research frontier and ethical/social impact

## When academic vs industry conflict

- Do not hide the conflict.
- Teach both.
- Explain when each applies.
- Use **exam answer**, **industry answer**, **research answer**, **field exception** labels.
