# Abstract

WAIKE: A Research Apprenticeship Operating Model for Community-Scale 6G Workforce Development presents a **research prototype** artifact for community-scale 6G research. Contribution focus: course-to-repo map, apprenticeship pathways, beginner→researcher flow.
Results reported here are from **preliminary toy demonstrations** using synthetic data only; field validation is future work.
We do not claim operational 6G deployment, Oulu/CWC affiliation, or carrier-grade performance.
